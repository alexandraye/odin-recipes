Macarons

Description

The most delicious macaron recipe ever! So tender and crispy, a go-to for any occaison!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Egg Whites
  • Almond Flour

Steps

  1. Line baking sheet with parchement paper.
  2. Beat sugar and egg whites together until fluffy.
  3. Sift powdered sugar and almond flour in another bowl.
  4. Fold together mixture.
  5. Pipe batter and let sit for 1 hour.
  6. Preheat oven to 200º and bake for 10 mins. Enjoy!
