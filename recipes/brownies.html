Brownies

Description

The most delicious brownie recipe ever! So tender and moist, a go-to for any occaison!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Salt
  • Semisweet Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350º and grease baking dish.
  2. Combine sugar, melted butter, and vanilla in a bowl.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  4. Sift flour, cocoa power and salt in another bowl.
  5. Add in flour mixture into egg mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Spread batter evenly into dish and bake for 40 mins. Enjoy!
