Cupcakes

Description

The most delicious cupcake recipe ever! So tender and moist, a go-to for any occaison!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350º and prepare cupcake tray.
  2. Beat sugar and butter together until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. Beat in vanilla extract.
  5. Add in flour mixture while alternating with milk.
  6. Fill tray with batter and bake for 20 mins. Enjoy!
